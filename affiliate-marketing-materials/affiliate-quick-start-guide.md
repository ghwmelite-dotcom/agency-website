## Commission Structure

### Project-Based Commissions
- **New Projects:** 10% of project value (up to $5,000)
- **Consulting Services:** 15% of service value
- **Recurring Maintenance:** 5% monthly (lifetime)

### Payment Timeline
- **Day 1:** Lead submits contact form via your link
- **Day 7-30:** Lead converts to client (project agreement signed)
- **Day 30-60:** Commission moves to "pending" (30-day refund period)
- **Day 60:** Commission approved and added to your payout balance
- **NET 30:** Payment processed monthly on the 1st

### Tier Benefits
| Tier | Requirement | Commission Boost | Perks |
|------|------------|------------------|-------|
| **Bronze** | 0-25 leads | 10% | Basic materials |
| **Silver** | 25+ leads OR 3+ conversions | +2% (12%) | Priority support |
| **Gold** | 100+ leads OR 10+ conversions | +5% (15%) | Custom landing pages |
| **Platinum** | Custom | Negotiated | Dedicated manager |

## Best Practices

### 1. Know Your Audience
**Best fit for OHWP Studios:**
- Startups building MVP (budget: $15K-$50K)
- SMBs modernizing systems (budget: $30K-$100K)
- Enterprises scaling tech (budget: $100K+)

**Red flags (don't waste time):**
- Businesses wanting a "$500 website"
- People looking for "cheap developers"
- Projects with unrealistic timelines
- Non-decision makers

### 2. Focus on Value, Not Selling
**Good approach:**
"I've been really impressed with OHWP's transparent pricing and timeline estimator. Here's a link if you want to check out what your project might cost: [link]"

**Bad approach:**
"You NEED to use OHWP Studios for your development! They're the best ever! Click here now!"

### 3. Be Transparent
**Always disclose:**
- You're an affiliate partner
- You earn commission if they sign up
- It doesn't cost them more

**Example disclosure:**
"Full disclosure: I'm an affiliate partner with OHWP Studios, which means I earn a commission if you work with them. That said, I only recommend them because I genuinely believe they do great work. No pressure either way!"

### 4. Target the Right Projects
**High-converting leads:**
- Custom web applications
- Mobile app development
- Enterprise software projects
- E-commerce platforms
- SaaS MVP development

**Lower-converting leads:**
- Simple WordPress sites
- Template-based solutions
- One-off landing pages
- Maintenance-only projects

### 5. Follow Up
- 3 days after sharing the link
- 1 week if no response
- Don't follow up more than 3 times

## FAQs

**Q: How long does the cookie last?**
A: 90 days. If someone clicks your link, we track them for 90 days.

**Q: Can I promote on paid ads?**
A: No. PPC ads (Google Ads, Facebook Ads) using "OHWP Studios" or brand terms are prohibited.

**Q: Can I create a review site?**
A: Yes! Just ensure it's honest and discloses affiliate relationship.

**Q: What if my referral uses multiple affiliate links?**
A: Last-click attribution. The most recent affiliate link gets credit.

**Q: Can I refer myself or my own company?**
A: No. Self-referrals are not eligible for commissions.

**Q: Is there a limit to how much I can earn?**
A: No limits! Top affiliates earn $5K-$10K per month.

**Q: What happens if a client requests a refund?**
A: Commission is reversed if refund happens within 30 days. After that, you keep the commission.

**Q: Can I see who converted from my link?**
A: For privacy reasons, no. You'll see conversion counts and commission amounts only.

**Q: How do I get promoted to Silver/Gold tier?**
A: Automatic. When you hit the threshold, you're auto-promoted.

**Q: Can multiple affiliates get credit for the same client?**
A: Only one affiliate per client (last-click wins).
