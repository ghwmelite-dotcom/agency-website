## Common Questions

### How long does the cookie last?

**90 days.** If someone clicks your link on January 1st and signs up on March 15th, you still get credit.

### Can I use paid ads?

No. **PPC campaigns using "OHWP Studios" or brand terms are prohibited.** You can use generic terms like "web development services" in ads.

### What if someone clicks multiple affiliate links?

**Last-click attribution.** Whoever's link they clicked most recently gets the commission.

### Is there a limit to how much I can earn?

**No.** We have no caps on total earnings, only per-project maximums ($5,000 for development projects).

### How do you track conversions?

We use **cookie-based tracking** + **form capture**. When someone clicks your link, we set a 90-day cookie. When they submit a contact form, we capture the affiliate code.

### What happens if a client requests a refund?

If a refund is issued within **30 days of project start**, the commission is reversed. After 30 days, you keep your commission.

### Can I see who converted?

For privacy reasons, no. You'll see conversion counts and commission amounts, but not client names.

### How do you prevent fraud?

We have multiple safeguards:
- IP-based rate limiting
- Manual review of high-value conversions
- Verification of lead quality
- Prohibition on self-referrals

### Can I recruit other affiliates?

Not yet, but a **2-tier program** (where you earn from your recruits) is coming soon.
