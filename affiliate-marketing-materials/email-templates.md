## Email Best Practices

### DO:
- Personalize each email with recipient's name and context
- Focus on solving THEIR problems, not selling
- Share genuine experiences and insights
- Keep emails concise (under 200 words)
- Include ONE clear call-to-action
- Follow up (but don't spam)

### DON'T:
- Send mass blast emails
- Make exaggerated claims
- Sound salesy or pushy
- Hide that you're an affiliate (be transparent)
- Email people who didn't opt-in
- Use ALL CAPS or excessive emojis

### Tracking Tips:
- Use different affiliate links for different campaigns
- Track open rates and click rates
- A/B test subject lines
- Note which templates perform best for your audience
- Follow up with non-openers after 3-5 days
