## Platform-Specific Best Practices

### LinkedIn
- **Optimal posting time:** Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM
- **Post length:** 150-300 words
- **Use:** 3-5 relevant hashtags
- **Engagement:** Comment on others' posts in your niche
- **Frequency:** 2-3x per week

### Twitter/X
- **Optimal posting time:** Monday-Friday, 12-3 PM
- **Tweet length:** Short and punchy (under 280 chars)
- **Use:** 1-2 hashtags max
- **Engagement:** Participate in relevant conversations
- **Frequency:** 1-3x per day

### Instagram
- **Optimal posting time:** Wednesday-Friday, 11 AM - 2 PM
- **Caption length:** 125-150 characters OR 2000+ (for stories)
- **Use:** 10-15 relevant hashtags
- **Engagement:** Respond to all comments within 1 hour
- **Frequency:** 3-5x per week

### Facebook
- **Optimal posting time:** Thursday-Friday, 1-3 PM
- **Post length:** 40-80 characters (short) OR ask questions
- **Use:** Minimal hashtags (1-2)
- **Engagement:** Create discussions, use polls
- **Frequency:** 1-2x per day
